Grid

The grid is based on flexbox. It distributes items evenly (.grid>.grid__item*x) or according to the classes set (.grid.grid--small-3>grid__item*x).

.grid the grid-container
.grid--gutter thereÂ´s a gutter between grid-items
.grid__item the grid-item
grid--{on this breakpoint and up}-{number of items} Example: .grid--huge-6
Meaning there are 6 items (cards) in a gutterless grid, 3 in a row in breakpoint small and upwards. Starting at breakpoint huge, there are 6 in a row.
Check the settings for grid columns: $grid-colums: 2 3 4 6; means only these grid-columns are generated.
